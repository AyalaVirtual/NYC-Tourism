New York

The Statue of Liberty with the New York City skyline in the distance.

I am in the great city of New York, weaving between the bustling traffic on the sidewalk as I try to navigate this concrete jungle. Although I've lived on Long Island all my life, I chose to spand this day experiencing New York as a tourist would.

The lights on the Brooklyn Bridge
New York City is known as the city that never sleeps.

New York City has a population of approximately 8.5 million people crowded within its limits, making it one of the most popular (and congested) places to live.

A street vendor selling hot dogs on the busy NYC sidewalk

New York City is a popular vacation spot, boasting a variety of attractions for tourists and native New Yorkers alike. There's something for everyone, and like they say, "If you can make it here, you can make it anywhere."

The dazzling New York City nightlife
